# 🦴 BoneSignal — Market Regime Engine

> *Most signals tell you what price is doing. BoneSignal tells you what the market is doing — and what to do about it.*

Built by **Bones HQ (@0x_BoneWalker)** for the BNB Hack: AI Trading Agent Edition — Track 2, Strategy Skills.

---

## The Problem With Generic TA

A bullish RSI crossover means something completely different in an Accumulation regime vs a Distribution regime. Traders who ignore market regime context get wrecked acting on signals that are technically correct but contextually wrong.

BoneSignal classifies the regime first. Then generates the signal.

---

## How It Works

BoneSignal pulls live data from the CoinMarketCap API across four layers and synthesizes them into a single regime classification with a specific actionable playbook.
